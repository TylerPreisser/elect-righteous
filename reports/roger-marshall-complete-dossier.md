## 5. THE FAUCI "MORON" INCIDENT & OTHER CONFRONTATIONS

### The Fauci Hot Mic Incident (January 11, 2022)

**What happened:** During a Senate HELP Committee hearing on COVID, Marshall questioned Dr. Anthony Fauci about his salary ($434,000/year in 2020) and financial disclosures, and whether Fauci would submit a financial disclosure including investments to Congress and the public. Fauci responded that his financial records were already publicly available.

**The hot mic moment:** When Marshall was cut off and another senator began questioning, Fauci's microphone caught him saying **"What a moron"** and **"Jesus Christ"** in exasperation.

**Video:** Yes -- the clip went viral, viewed nearly 3 million times.

**Marshall's response:** Tweeted: "Calling me a moron during a Senate hearing may have alleviated the stress of the least trusted bureaucrat in America, but it didn't take away from the facts. We need the truth Dr. Fauci!"

**Legislative response:** Marshall introduced the **FAUCI Act** (Financial Accountability for Uniquely Compensated Individuals) to require public posting of financial disclosures for high-ranking government officials on the Office of Government Ethics website.

**Follow-up:** Marshall obtained and published Fauci's previously unpublished financial disclosures from NIH, claimed Fauci had been misleading about their public availability. (Note: Fact-checkers found Fauci's disclosures were technically publicly available via FOIA request, but were not posted online.)

### The Oakley Town Hall Walkout (March 1, 2025)

**What happened:** Marshall held a town hall at a hospital in Oakley, Kansas (Logan County -- 85% Trump in 2024). Expected 20-30 people; the crowd was so large they had to move to a larger space.

**The confrontation:** Constituents immediately began peppering Marshall with angry questions about DOGE cuts, the Ukraine conflict, hollowing of federal agencies, and rural healthcare. The crowd booed and shouted.

**The exit:** Marshall left approximately 20 minutes early, reportedly calling the audience "rude" and saying "God bless America" as he bolted for the door. He was **called a "coward"** as he departed. He stated he had "two more commitments today."

**Marshall's response:** His chief of staff claimed "Democrat operatives that couldn't place Oakley, Kansas on a map before today sabotaged a local town hall." Marshall later called attendees "paid Democratic operatives."

**Public pushback:** Kansas residents who attended pushed back hard, saying they were real constituents concerned about agriculture funding cuts and federal worker layoffs. One Kansas Reflector columnist wrote: "If he proves it, I'll eat a copy of Project 2025."

### CNN Confrontations (2025-2026)
- **Iran nuclear comments:** CNN's Kaitlan Collins confronted Marshall about his claim that Iran wouldn't pose a nuclear threat for years. Marshall admitted on air: **"Look, I was wrong."**
- **Trump AI video:** Collins asked Marshall about a controversial AI video Trump posted; Marshall deflected.
