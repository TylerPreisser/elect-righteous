# Faith, Church & Religious Community Investigation
## Hays/Ellis County Officials + Kansas Statewide Candidates
### Investigation Date: March 31, 2026

---

## INVESTIGATION METHODOLOGY

For each official, the following search vectors were used:
- Direct name + church/faith/Christian/denomination searches
- Candidate profiles on HaysPost, Ballotpedia, campaign websites
- Social media (Facebook, about.me, LinkedIn)
- Church directories, Knights of Columbus databases
- Obituary/family records (often name parish affiliations)
- Campaign questionnaires, candidate forums
- News articles referencing faith positions

**Confidence ratings:**
- **CONFIRMED** = Named church/denomination found in official profile, news article, or candidate bio
- **STRONG INDICATOR** = Family/school ties strongly suggest affiliation but no direct membership statement found
- **INFERRED** = Circumstantial evidence only (German-Catholic surname, attended Catholic school, etc.)
- **NO PUBLIC RECORD** = Nothing found in public sources
