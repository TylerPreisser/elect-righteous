## Jurisdiction
- **Level**: State (Supreme Court, Court of Appeals) and Local (District Court)
- **District/Ward**: Statewide for Supreme Court and Court of Appeals; 23rd Judicial District for district judges
- 23rd Judicial District covers: Ellis County, Gove County, Sheridan County, and Trego County
- **Eligible Voters**: All registered Kansas voters (Supreme Court/COA); voters in the judicial district (district judges)

## How Retention Works
- Judges appear on the ballot with a simple question: "Shall [Judge Name] be retained in office?"
- Voters vote YES or NO
- A majority YES vote retains the judge; majority NO removes them
- Court of Appeals and district judges stand for retention every 4 years
- Supreme Court justices stand for retention every 6 years
- There are no opposing candidates -- it is purely a yes/no retention vote

## Declared Candidates
- N/A -- Retention elections do not have candidates. Judges are either retained or not.
