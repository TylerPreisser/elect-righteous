# 23rd Judicial District Court Elections (Ellis, Gove, Rooks, Trego Counties)

## Election Details
- **Date**: November 3, 2026 (General Election) with August 4, 2026 Primary
- **Type**: Partisan Election (the 23rd Judicial District is one of 14 Kansas districts that elect judges via partisan ballot, NOT retention)
- **Jurisdiction**: 23rd Judicial District (Ellis, Gove, Rooks, Trego counties)

## How 23rd District Judges Are Selected
- **District Judges**: Partisan election (primary + general), 4-year terms
- **District Magistrate Judges**: In partisan-election districts, magistrate judges also run in partisan elections for 4-year terms
- Filing deadline: June 1, 2026 (Kansas Secretary of State)
- Primary: August 4, 2026
- General: November 3, 2026
