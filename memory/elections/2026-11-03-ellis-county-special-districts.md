## 5. K-State Research & Extension - Ellis County

### Status: ANNUAL MEETING ELECTION (NOT ON BALLOT)
- The County Extension Council is composed of 24 members organized into 4 Program Development Committees (Agriculture, Family & Consumer Sciences, 4-H Youth Development, Economic/Community Development)
- At the annual meeting, the Extension Council elects from its own members 9 people to serve on the Executive Board (chair, vice chair, secretary, treasurer, + 5 members)
- All citizens of voting age are eligible to vote in the annual meeting elections
- **Not on the general election ballot** - elected at the annual meeting
- **Contact**: Ellis County Extension Office (https://www.ellis.k-state.edu/about/extension-council.html)

### Confidence: HIGH (structure confirmed)
