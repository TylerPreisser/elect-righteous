# Hays USD 489 School Board -- Next Election November 2027

## Status: NO 2026 ELECTION

Kansas school board elections occur in odd-numbered years (November). The most recent USD 489 school board election was November 2025 (results not fully captured in research). The previous election in 2023 had 10 candidates for available seats.

## Next Election
- **November 2027** -- Staggered terms mean either 3 or 4 seats will be up.
- Kansas school board elections: general election is the Tuesday following the first Monday in November of odd-numbered years. Primary (if needed) is the first Tuesday in August.

## Board Structure
- 7 members serving 4-year staggered terms
- Approximately 3 seats up one odd year, 4 seats the following odd year
