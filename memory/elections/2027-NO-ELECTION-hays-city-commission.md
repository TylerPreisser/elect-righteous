# Hays City Commission -- Next Election November 2027

## Status: NO 2026 ELECTION

Hays city commission elections occur in odd-numbered years (November). The most recent election was November 2025, where:
- Shaun Musil won a 4-year term (1,772 votes)
- David Vilaysing won a 4-year term (1,340 votes)
- Sandy Jacobs won a 2-year term (1,542 votes)

## Next Election
- **November 2027** -- The 2-year seat (Sandy Jacobs) plus potentially other seats will be on the ballot.
- Kansas municipal elections are held on the Tuesday following the first Monday in November in odd-numbered years.

## City Government Structure
- Hays operates under a commission-manager form of government
- 5-member City Commission elected at-large
- Commissioners serve staggered terms (mix of 2-year and 4-year)
