## Notes
- This is a SAFE REPUBLICAN seat (R+16). The general election is very unlikely to be competitive.
- Tracey Mann appears to be running unopposed in the Republican primary.
- Hays, Kansas is squarely in KS-01, which covers the vast western/central portion of the state.
- The district is geographically the largest in Kansas, covering most of the western half of the state.
- Key issues for KS-01 voters: agriculture policy, water rights (Ogallala Aquifer depletion), rural healthcare, immigration.
