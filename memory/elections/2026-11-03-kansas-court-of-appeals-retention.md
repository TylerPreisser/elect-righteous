# Kansas Court of Appeals Retention Elections

## Election Details
- **Date**: November 3, 2026 (General Election)
- **Type**: Judicial Retention (Yes/No vote)
- **Jurisdiction**: Statewide (all Kansas voters including Hays/Ellis County)

## Additional Judges Possible
- Any Court of Appeals judges appointed since November 2024 who have served at least one year by November 2026 would also appear on the ballot for their first retention vote. This list may grow as appointments are made.

## How It Works
- Court of Appeals judges are appointed by the Governor, confirmed by the Kansas Senate
- Must stand for retention in the next even-year general election after serving at least 1 year
- If retained, serve 4-year terms with retention every 4 years
- May serve until age 70
- Simple majority "No" vote removes the judge
