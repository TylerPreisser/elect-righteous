# Kansas Governor's Race 2026

## Key Dates
- **Filing Deadline**: June 1, 2026 at noon
- **Primary**: August 4, 2026
- **General Election**: November 3, 2026
- **Registration Deadline**: ~October 13, 2026

## Position(s)
- Governor of Kansas + Lieutenant Governor (ticket) — 1 seat
- Current incumbent: **Laura Kelly (D)** — TERM-LIMITED, cannot run again
- **This is an OPEN SEAT race**

## Jurisdiction
- **Level**: State
- **District**: Statewide (all Kansas voters)

## Also on Ballot (Statewide)
All statewide executive offices are on the 2026 ballot:
- Attorney General
- Secretary of State (open if Schwab runs for Gov)
- State Treasurer (Steven Johnson, R — incumbent)
- Insurance Commissioner (Vicki Schmidt, R — Daniel Hawkins (R) and Dinah Sykes (D) announced)

## Significance
- Open seat = most competitive type of race
- Governor Kelly (D) won in traditionally Republican Kansas TWICE
- Schwab running for Gov means Secretary of State is also an open seat
- If Kobach runs for Gov, Attorney General also becomes open
- Could be a reshuffling of multiple statewide offices in one cycle
