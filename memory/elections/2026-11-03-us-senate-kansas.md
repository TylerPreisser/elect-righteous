## Position(s)
- U.S. Senator from Kansas (Class II seat) -- 1 seat
- Current incumbent: **Roger Marshall (R)** -- first elected 2020 (53.2% of vote)
- Term length: 6 years
- Note: Jerry Moran (R) holds the other Kansas Senate seat (Class III) -- not up until 2028

## Jurisdiction
- **Level**: Federal
- **District/Ward**: Statewide
- **Eligible Voters**: All registered Kansas voters

## Declared Candidates

### Republican Primary
1. **Roger Marshall** (incumbent) -- First-term senator. Former U.S. Representative (KS-01). Physician (OB-GYN). Has stated intent to run for re-election.
2. **Chase LaPorte** -- Republican challenger. Limited public profile information available.

### Democratic Primary
1. **Jason Hart** -- Recently resigned as Assistant United States Attorney for the District of Kansas. Specialized in child exploitation and cybercrime cases.
2. **Christy Cauble Davis** -- Former Kansas director for USDA Rural Development under Biden. Ran unsuccessfully for KS-01 in 2020.
3. **Sandy Spidel Neumann** -- Retired financial services executive (20+ years at Ameriprise). No prior public office.
4. **Anne Parelkar** -- Immigration attorney. No prior public office.
5. **Erik Murray** -- Real estate developer.
