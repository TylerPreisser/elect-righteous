## Methodology

Platforms surveyed: campaign website, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, Truth Social, Gab, Gettr, Substack, Reddit, and TikTok. Candidate-controlled item count: 17 substantive original items captured, consisting of 15 YouTube videos and 2 dated campaign website updates, plus 1 undated launch placeholder recorded as presence evidence. Profile-level checks were captured for Facebook, Instagram, X, and LinkedIn. No candidate-controlled account was found on Truth Social, Gab, Gettr, Substack, Reddit, or TikTok.

We report patterns; we do not interpret intent.
