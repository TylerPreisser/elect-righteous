## CODEX DEEP RESEARCH ROUND 2 2026-04-01

### Verified Identity
- Charlotte O'Hara is a former Johnson County commissioner, former Kansas House member, and a 2026 Republican gubernatorial candidate.

### Deep Biography
- Campaign website: [oharaforkansas.com](https://oharaforkansas.com/)
- Her biography says she was raised on a farm in Bourbon County, graduated from KU, spent over 20 years as a general contractor, homeschooled two youngest children, and now co-owns a small manufacturing company in Olathe with two sons.
- The campaign page frames her as a proven conservative with a long record in local and state politics.

### Record / Votes / Actions
- O'Hara says that during COVID she refused masks and vaccines and opposed school and business closures while serving on the Johnson County Commission.
- She also served in the Kansas House and as a Republican district chairwoman and precinct committeewoman.
- Her public brand is centered on life, property rights, education, election integrity, and free-market politics.
