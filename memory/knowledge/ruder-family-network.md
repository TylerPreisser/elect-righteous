## Significance
One family simultaneously holds elected positions in:
1. City government (Mayor — the highest city position)
2. County government (Treasurer — handles all county finances)
3. School district governance (Board Vice President — oversees $93.5M budget)

This gives the Ruder family influence over three separate taxing jurisdictions that cover the same population. While there is no evidence of coordination or impropriety, voters should be aware of this concentration of political influence in one family.
